Salon Booking Flow Demo

This video demo page is designed for BookedAI.au and follows a customer who needs a wedding haircut tomorrow. The flow shows enquiry, available options, slot selection, and a confirmed booking with BookedAI.

Storyboard

  • Customer asks for a haircut booking needed tomorrow for a wedding.
  • BookedAI replies instantly with clear available time options.
  • The customer selects a preferred slot in a guided flow.
  • BookedAI confirms the booking and closes the conversation clearly.

OpenAI Demo Prompt

from openai import OpenAI

client = OpenAI()

video = client.videos.create(
    model="sora-2",
    prompt="Create a 30-second style BookedAI demo for a salon customer who needs a wedding haircut tomorrow. Show the assistant presenting booking options, the customer selecting a time, and the booking being confirmed with BookedAI.",
    size="1280x720",
    seconds=20,
)
