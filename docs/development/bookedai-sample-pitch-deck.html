01
Problem

Most service businesses do not have a lead problem. They have a conversion systems problem.

Fragmented intake

Web chat, forms, DMs, and manual follow-up split intent across tools.

Weak handoff

Inquiry context gets lost between qualification, scheduling, and CRM updates.

No operator truth

Teams lack one surface for pipeline, bookings, sync posture, and payment visibility.

02
Insight

The real product is not “AI chat”. The real product is revenue continuity from first question to booked outcome.

03
Solution

BookedAI creates one flow from conversation to revenue operations.

Customer side

  • AI assistant answers questions and captures intent
  • Live booking-aware paths guide users into action
  • Public product surfaces support hosted and embedded entry points

Operator side

  • Admin workspace for leads, customers, bookings, services, payments
  • Tenant, role, settings, and audit control plane
  • CRM sync visibility and recovery posture
04

Integration layer
