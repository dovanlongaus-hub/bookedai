## Summary

Upgraded BookedAI messaging so SME copy leads with missed enquiries and booked revenue, while investor/judge copy frames BookedAI as an omnichannel AI Revenue Engine with auditable revenue actions and clear financial logic.

## Details

Applied the selected stronger messages across project.md, prd.md, README.md, DESIGN.md, the master PRD, the master roadmap, the current phase/sprint plan, the next-phase implementation plan, implementation progress, and memory. The new SME-facing hero is: Never lose a service enquiry to slow replies again. The new market-facing message is: BookedAI turns missed service enquiries into booked revenue, captures intent across chat/calls/email/web/messaging apps, then helps book, follow up, track payment posture, and show operators what revenue was won or still needs action. The investor/judge narrative now frames BookedAI as an AI Revenue Engine: an omnichannel agent layer that captures intent, creates booking references, tracks payment and follow-up posture, and records every revenue action in an auditable operating system. Financial clarity is now explicit: setup fee, SaaS subscription, and performance-aligned commission or revenue share where appropriate, backed by booking references, payment posture, portal reopen, tenant Ops, audit/action evidence, and release gates. The omnichannel requirement was also tightened to avoid overclaiming instant payment collection or lifecycle completion before backend state confirms it.
