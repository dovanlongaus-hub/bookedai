# BookedAI designer brief, one page

## Project
BookedAI promo video, keyframe design pass

## Deliverable
Create 8 keyframes for a 35 to 40 second promo video before motion design or editing starts.

## Objective
Show BookedAI as an AI revenue engine for service businesses, from inbound enquiry to booking flow to operator visibility.

## Audience
- service business operators
- agency owners
- early customers
- investors and partners

## Core message
BookedAI turns inbound questions and service interest into structured revenue operations, from AI conversation to booking flow, CRM sync, payments, and operator control.

## Visual direction
- premium dark SaaS aesthetic
- cyan and violet accents
- subtle glassmorphism
- calm confidence, not flashy hype
- clean typography, sparse layouts
- product-first, not stock-photo-first

## Output format
- primary: 16:9
- design for 1080p composition
- each frame should work as a strong standalone still

## 8 keyframes to design
1. **Problem**  
   Disconnected signals: search, calls, emails, enquiries.  
   Text: `Service businesses do not need more tools.`

2. **Category framing**  
   Signals converge into one system or one flow.  
   Text: `They need one revenue flow.`

3. **AI conversation capture**  
   Mobile assistant UI as the first intelligent touchpoint.  
   Text: `AI conversation captures intent.`

4. **Bookable action**  
   Available appointment times and service match.  
   Text: `Intent becomes bookable action.`

5. **Booking outcome**  
   Confirmation state with appointment details.  
   Text: `Booking confirmed.`

6. **Full workflow**  
   Desktop product workflow showing search, results, and booking path.  
   Text: `From inquiry to booking flow.`

7. **Operator control**  
   Control-center style composition for CRM sync, payments, tenants, and audit.  
   Text: `CRM sync. Payments. Tenants. Audit.`

8. **Brand close**  
   BookedAI logo and premium final frame.  
   Text: `BookedAI, AI revenue engine for service businesses.`

## Design rules
- one message per frame
- preserve UI legibility
- avoid noisy generic dashboard filler
- keep overlays short and readable
- effects should support clarity, not dominate the frame
- final result should feel investment-grade and product-real

## What to avoid
- meme editing style
- too many floating widgets
- overuse of neon glow
- generic AI robot imagery
- oversized paragraphs on screen

## Success criteria
A viewer should understand in under 40 seconds that:
- BookedAI starts at AI conversation,
- converts intent into booking,
- and extends into real operator-grade revenue workflow.
