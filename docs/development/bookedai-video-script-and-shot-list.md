# BookedAI video script and shot list

## Project
BookedAI promo video

## Goal
Create a polished 35 to 45 second promo video that explains BookedAI as an AI revenue engine for service businesses.

## Audience
- service business operators
- agency owners
- early customers
- partners and investors

## Format
- primary: 16:9
- optional cutdown: 9:16
- runtime target: 40 seconds
- delivery: 1080p MP4

## Tone
- premium
- modern
- calm confidence
- AI-native, but business-first

## Visual style
- dark premium SaaS look
- cyan and violet accents
- glassmorphism overlays
- fast but readable UI movement
- minimal clutter, strong typography

## Core message
BookedAI turns inbound questions and service interest into structured revenue operations, from AI search and conversation to booking flow, CRM sync, payments, reminders, retention actions, tenant financial summaries, and operator visibility.

---

# Final voiceover script

Service businesses do not need another disconnected tool.
They need one revenue flow.
BookedAI captures customer intent through AI search and conversation,
turns it into bookings, follow-up, reminders, and customer care,
while a revenue operations agent keeps CRM sync, payments, billing, retention, and audit moving.
BookedAI is the AI revenue engine for service businesses.

# Edit structure

## Rhythm
- 0 to 7s: hook and framing
- 7 to 21s: conversation to booking proof
- 21 to 33s: broader workflow and operator value
- 33 to 40s: brand close

## Transitions
- fade from black at open
- mostly hard cuts or soft push transitions
- avoid flashy zooms that obscure UI legibility
- use motion blur sparingly

## Animation notes
- text reveal should be 200 to 350ms
- screenshot pans should be slow and deliberate
- glows should feel premium, not neon-gamer heavy

---

# Music direction

## Music brief
- electronic cinematic pulse
- modern but restrained
- should feel premium and forward-moving
- avoid over-dramatic trailer sound

## Music flow
- first 7 seconds: atmospheric build
- middle 7 to 30 seconds: steady pulse under UI proof
- last 8 seconds: subtle lift for brand close
