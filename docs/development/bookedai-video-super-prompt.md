# BookedAI video super-prompt

Use this as a single master prompt for a video creator, AI editor, or production assistant.

---

Create a polished 40-second 1080p promo video for **BookedAI** in **16:9** format.

## Product positioning
BookedAI is an **AI revenue engine for service businesses**. It turns inbound questions and service interest into structured revenue operations, from AI conversation to booking flow, CRM sync, payments, and operator visibility.

## Audience
- service business operators
- agency owners
- early customers
- investors and partners

## Tone
- premium
- modern
- calm confidence
- AI-native, but business-first
- not hype-heavy
- not meme-like

## Visual style
Use a **premium dark SaaS** visual direction with:
- cyan and violet accents
- subtle glassmorphism
- clean typography
- strong contrast
- minimal clutter
- polished product-marketing composition
- cinematic restraint
- elegant motion, not flashy motion

Avoid:
- generic robot imagery
- stock-photo people
- cluttered fake dashboards
- excessive neon glow
- aggressive zoom spam
- noisy transitions

## Core message
BookedAI helps service businesses move from disconnected enquiries to one revenue flow, starting with AI conversation and ending in booking, operator visibility, and revenue operations control.

Use this exact narration or a close professional read with the same meaning:

"Service businesses do not need another disconnected tool.

## Text overlay rules
- one idea per scene
- keep each line short and readable
- preserve lower-third or center-safe space depending on UI crop
- typography should feel premium and minimal

## Editing rules
- use hard cuts or soft push transitions
- keep screenshot movement slow and deliberate
- do not obscure UI with heavy effects
- prioritize clarity over spectacle
- build momentum, but stay calm and controlled

## Music brief
- electronic cinematic pulse
- premium and modern
- not over-dramatic
- soft build in first 7 seconds
- steady pulse during product proof
- subtle lift in final brand close
