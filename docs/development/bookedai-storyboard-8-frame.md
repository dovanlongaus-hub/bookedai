# BookedAI storyboard, 8 frames

## Purpose
Storyboard draft for a designer to create keyframes before motion or editing.

## Format
- primary output: 16:9
- visual style: premium dark SaaS
- accent colors: cyan and violet
- mood: calm confidence, modern, AI-native, business-first

---

## Frame 1, The problem
**Time range:** 0.0 to 3.5s

**Goal:** Open with the pain clearly.

**Visual concept:**
- dark gradient background
- floating fragments representing search, calls, emails, and enquiries
- fragmented signals feel scattered and disconnected
- one glass panel in the center trying to organize the chaos

**On-frame text:**
Service businesses do not need more tools.

**Design notes:**
- keep it minimal, cinematic, and not too literal
- signals can be simple icons or abstract UI chips
- avoid clutter, focus on one strong centered composition

**Motion hint:**
- light parallax drift
- soft fade-in from black

---

## Frame 2, One revenue flow
**Time range:** 3.5 to 7.0s

**Goal:** Reframe the product category.

**Visual concept:**
- use the strongest cropped area from `output/playwright/root-desktop.png`
- emphasize the value proposition region
- show the scattered signals visually converging into one direction or flow line

**On-frame text:**
They need one revenue flow.

**Design notes:**
- crop tightly, do not use the whole long page at once
- allow one strong directional line or glow to unify the scene

**Motion hint:**
- slow push-in
- subtle convergence animation

---

## Frame 3, AI conversation capture
**Time range:** 7.0 to 12.0s

**Goal:** Show where BookedAI starts creating value.

**Visual concept:**
- use `output/playwright/product-compact-selected-summary.png`
- mobile assistant interface centered in frame
- input field or live indicator highlighted
- query state feels active and responsive

**On-frame text:**
AI conversation captures intent.

**Design notes:**
- keep the mobile UI sharp and legible
- use glow sparingly around the active area
- make the frame feel focused and intelligent, not noisy

**Motion hint:**
- pulse on input area
- subtle zoom-in

---

## Frame 4, Intent becomes bookable
**Time range:** 12.0 to 17.0s

**Goal:** Prove AI turns into action.

**Visual concept:**
- use `frontend/public/bookedai-ui-shot-1.jpg`
- available appointment times are the main visual anchor
- one chosen time slot gets a cyan highlight

**On-frame text:**
Intent becomes bookable action.

**Design notes:**
- emphasize clarity, not quantity of UI
- crop to the region where time selection feels obvious
- viewer should instantly understand that the system is moving toward a booking

**Motion hint:**
- one slot highlight sweep
- smooth pan across times

---

## Frame 5, Booking confirmed
**Time range:** 17.0 to 21.0s

**Goal:** Show the successful outcome.

**Visual concept:**
- use `frontend/public/bookedai-ui-shot-2.jpg`
- focus on the confirmation state and appointment detail block
- add a soft success glow or confirmation check emphasis

**On-frame text:**
Booking confirmed.

**Design notes:**
- this frame should feel stable and reassuring
- avoid extra effects here, let certainty do the work

**Motion hint:**
- near-static hold
- tiny confirmation pulse only

---

## Frame 6, Full workflow on desktop
**Time range:** 21.0 to 27.0s

**Goal:** Expand from one booking moment to the broader workflow.

**Visual concept:**
- use `frontend/output/local-homepage-qa/homepage-desktop.png`
- crop across search, results, and booking areas
- show a connected left-to-right product story within one screen

**On-frame text:**
From inquiry to booking flow.

**Design notes:**
- frame should feel like a serious product, not a landing-page mockup
- keep key product regions readable
- avoid showing too much tiny text

**Motion hint:**
- controlled horizontal pan
- gentle depth separation between panels

---

## Frame 7, Operator visibility
**Time range:** 27.0 to 33.0s

**Goal:** Show that BookedAI is more than the front-end assistant.

**Visual concept:**
- compose a tiled operator frame
- use a branded dark panel layout with 4 tiles:
  - CRM sync
  - payments
  - tenants
  - audit
- if real admin screenshots are not ready, use clean stylized UI placeholders that match BookedAI colors

**On-frame text:**
CRM sync. Payments. Tenants. Audit.

**Design notes:**
- this should feel like a control center
- make tiles balanced and premium
- avoid fake data tables that look noisy or generic

**Motion hint:**
- tile-by-tile reveal
- light glow on active tile states

---

## Frame 8, Brand close
**Time range:** 33.0 to 40.0s

**Goal:** Land the product identity clearly.

**Visual concept:**
- dark hero background
- BookedAI logo centered or slightly above center
- optional soft collage of blurred UI frames behind it
- final line appears under logo

**On-frame text:**
BookedAI
AI revenue engine for service businesses.

**Design notes:**
- keep the close elegant and clean
- logo must be the visual hero
- final frame should feel investment-grade, not ad-noisy

**Motion hint:**
- smooth logo resolve
- subtle ambient glow lift at the end
