# BookedAI SME Customer Guide

## Audience

This guide is for the SME customer using BookedAI as a business solution. In this context, the SME customer is the business owner or business team relying on the platform to capture leads, manage services, and increase bookings.

## What the SME Customer Cares About

- more bookings from conversations
- reduced response delay
- cleaner lead capture
- reliable booking handoff
- service catalog accuracy
- visibility into recent activity

## Platform Value for SME Customers

### Lead capture

BookedAI can capture inbound enquiry context from web chat and supported automation pathways.

### Service matching

The assistant recommends services from the business catalog rather than generic or invented options.

### Workflow handoff

Booking flows can trigger downstream automation through n8n.

### Payment and scheduling support

The platform can support:

- Stripe checkout links
- consultation checkout links
- Zoho meeting creation
- email confirmations

### Business visibility

The SME customer can inspect:

- recent bookings
- recent events
- service records
- partner records
- system configuration visibility from admin tools

## Core Business Flows

### Maintain service catalog

Business users can:

- import services from a business website
- manage services in admin
- keep recommendations aligned with the current offer set

### Monitor bookings

Business users can:

- review booking records
- inspect event timelines
- manually send confirmation emails when needed

### Manage brand and trust surfaces

Business users can:

- manage partner records
- maintain public-facing content alignment

## Business Dependencies

SME customer outcomes depend on:

- accurate service data
- working email channels
- healthy workflow callback behavior
- consistent AI extraction
- correct environment configuration

## Business Risks to Watch

- outdated service catalog leads to poor recommendations
- partial automation without documentation creates operational confusion
- changed pricing or payment flows without frontend and email updates create trust issues
- webhook or callback mismatch causes silent workflow failure

## Documentation Maintenance Rule

If business operations, booking handling, payment behavior, or service-catalog workflows change, this document must be reviewed and updated.
